
============================ INFO ============================

These are cuctom-made Python-Layers in Caffe. They require you to have compile 
PyCaffe with PythonLayer parameter enabled !!!

Also you have to append the location you store the file into your PYTHONPATH eviroment variable for this to work.

    ----  Methods ----

    	  - Realtime stostastic data-augmentation ---> currently works with batch size equal to 1. 
	    Augmentation hyperpameters are definied inside the the PYTHONLAYER and not in the prototxt
