
========================== INFO ==========================

This folder contained the pre-trained models trained using the 
VGG16-FCN weights from [1]. The architecture is been modified 
according to [2] with some small modifications.





	  [1] Long, J., Shelhamer, E., & Darrell, T. (2015). Fully convolutional networks for semantic
    	      segmentation. In Proceedings of the IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern 
    	      Recognition (pp. 3431-3440).


	 [2] Marmanis, D., Schindler, K., Wegner, J. D., Galliani, S., Datcu, M., & Stilla, U. (2016). 
	     Classification with an edge: improving semantic image segmentation with boundary detection. 
	      arXiv preprint arXiv:1612.01337.
